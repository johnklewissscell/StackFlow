About StackFlow


StackFlow is a stock simulator made by 15 year old, Owen Forloine, in the hopes of helping others learn about the stock market in a fun and interactive way. It leverages AI to provide users with insights and guidance, making it easier to understand market trends and make informed decisions. Owen and his colleague, Amir Mechkour hope to make StackFlow used by people of all ages as a fun, introductory way to get handy with trading and buying/selling shares of stocks. We believe finance is important, and everyone should have the chance to get comfortable with it without having to always use real money or pay for a simulator.

We collectively believe that if you get yourself into financing with StackFlow, you will gradually gain the confidence and skill to go towards the real stock market. Just remember our motto, time is money. We hope you enjoy this as much as we do, and happy investing!
