About StackFlow


StackFlow is a stock simulator made by 15 year old, Owen Forloine in the hopes of helping others learn about the stock market in a fun and interactive way. It leverages AI to provide users with insights and guidance, making it easier to understand market trends and make informed decisions. Owen and his colleague, Amir Mechkour hope to make StackFlow used by people of all ages as a fun, interactive way to get handy with trading and buying/selling shares of stocks. We believe finance is important, and everyone should have the chance to get comfortable with it without having to always use real money or pay for a simulator.
